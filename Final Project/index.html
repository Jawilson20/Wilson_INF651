About Playbook Simulator

The Playbook Simulator is an interactive tool designed to help coaches, players, and fans design and simulate football plays. With drag-and-drop functionality and real-time feedback, you can create strategies and visualize how they unfold on the field.

Features include:

  • Interactive playfield
  • Player positioning
  • Save and load plays
